## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Inbox cards omit redundant ACE pillar explainer

Dashboard inbox cards SHALL NOT display the copy: `ACE pillars — brighter when that section has written observations (not GAINS alone).`

#### Scenario: Inbox card rendered

- **WHEN** a user views an inbox card that previously showed the ACE pillar explainer line above the pillar chips
- **THEN** that explainer sentence SHALL NOT appear (pill chips and other content MAY remain)
